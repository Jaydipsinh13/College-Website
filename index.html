Course We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science courses for students by Thakur College of Engineering Technology

BE

The BE course is a full-time four-year Bachelor's Degree in Engineering. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Engineering.

BTech

BTech (Bachelor of Technology) is about technology and its applications. This degree primarily focuses on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

BVoC (Vocational)

The Bachelor of Vocation (BVoC) is an undergraduate degree course in vocational studies for a duration of 3 years. The field relates to the design, development, and use of vocational applications.
